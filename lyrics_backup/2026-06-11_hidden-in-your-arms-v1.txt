[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I dim the lantern low and pull you close
Tracing every secret only midnight knows
You lean a little nearer, catching my eye
Just a pair of wild souls underneath the sky

[Verse 1]
We left the gravel road a million miles behind
Just to see what kind of heaven we could finally find
Your fingers brush my collar, dragging me right in
Right where all my wandering ends and you begin

[Pre-Chorus]
The embers are glowing in the cool dark air
There is a sweet kind of magic in the way you stare
I can feel the heavy gravity pulling me down deep
A promise that my lonely heart is ready to keep

[Chorus]
I am hidden in your arms, nowhere else to be
Lost inside the beautiful way you look at me
With every single heartbeat, I am calling out your name
I am hidden in your arms, melting in the flame

[Post-Chorus]
(Hidden in your arms) Let the whole world fade
(Hidden in your arms) In the memory we made

[Verse 2]
You hum a quiet melody against my chest
In this little sanctuary where we come to rest
The scent of pine and rainfall lingers in the breeze
You bring me down completely, falling to my knees

[Bridge]
I am hidden in your arms, nowhere else to be
